# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Potential clients evaluating whether to hire Lucas-Adebayo Daniel for full-stack or AI-engineering work. They arrive with a specific problem (product to build, system to automate, AI integration needed) and scan for proof of technical depth, design sensibility, and reliability. They are often non-technical founders or product managers who may not read every detail but will feel the quality of the interface instantly.

## Product Purpose

A personal portfolio that demonstrates technical capability, design craft, and problem-solving depth through showcased projects, writing, and an exceptional interface experience. Success is measured by inbound client inquiries, project opportunities, and the immediate sense of trust the site creates within the first 10 seconds.

## Brand Personality

**Polished, Technical, Confident**

- **Polished**: Every pixel, transition, and interaction feels considered. No rough edges.
- **Technical**: The interface itself is a demonstration of engineering skill. Three.js, motion, and precise layout are part of the proof.
- **Confident**: Expert but approachable. The work speaks before the copy does. No need for hyperbole or buzzwords.

Voice is calm, direct, and human. Write like a skilled practitioner talking to a smart collaborator, not a marketer pitching a prospect.

## Anti-references

- Corporate career pages (impersonal, templated, forgettable).
- Generic SaaS landing page clichés (big metric counters, identical icon grids, gradient-text hero slogans).
- Over-designed portfolios where effects compete for attention and nothing has breathing room.
- "AI slop" aesthetics: neon-on-black crypto vibes, glassmorphism used as decoration, side-stripe accent borders.

## Design Principles

1. **Practice what you preach** — the portfolio interface should demonstrate the same technical excellence as the projects it showcases.
2. **Show, don't tell** — let the quality of motion, typography, and interaction prove capability before the user reads a single description.
3. **Expert confidence, not arrogance** — polished craft that invites trust without shouting. Understated beats loud.
4. **Motion with meaning** — every animation serves wayfinding, hierarchy, or genuine delight. No decoration for its own sake.
5. **Personal, not corporate** — warmth and individuality come through in micro-copy, detail, and unconventional choices. Avoid safe defaults.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Target WCAG 2.1 AA as a baseline.
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`: disable or simplify scroll-driven and Three.js animations when the user requests reduced motion.
- Ensure sufficient contrast in both light and dark themes.
- Keyboard-navigable interactive elements (project cards, navigation, theme toggle).
